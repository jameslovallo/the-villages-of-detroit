Your generous financial support helps us to continue working on the behalf of all those who live in the Villages of Detroit. From breaking down barriers between and within neighborhoods to assisting with fighting housing insecurity in the Villages, we are committed to lifting up all of our residents and communities. Some of our services include advocating for neighborhood infrastructure upgrades, supporting community led programming efforts, lobbying the City for residential needs and connecting folks with resources or just generally serving as a sounding board. We look forward to expanding our reach and capacity in the coming months, and we will be able to do so with your support.
